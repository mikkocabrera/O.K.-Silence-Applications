About the Project

A new heavy project with room for weight, melody, and emotion.

O.K Silence is a new NYC based heavy music project blending nu metal, emo, metalcore, and Midwest emo influences. The sound should feel atmospheric, heavy, emotional, and dynamic, with tight rhythmic sections, melodic contrast, and space for both aggression and vulnerability.
